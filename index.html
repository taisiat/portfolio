About

I am Taisia (TK!)

Actively writing code and building solutions.

Software engineer and jack-of-all-trades who understands that there is no substitute for deeply understanding the users and how the app/solution fits into their lives.

Across diverse roles - from management consultant to early-stage AI startup jack-of-all-trades - TK has bridged the gap between engineers and users to deliver solutions that users love. She strives to amplify the positive impact that each user can have by building solutions that scale. In parallel, TK believes that effective collaboration amplifies a team's impact. She's currently open to software engineering opportunities on user-centric teams that drive impact collaboratively.
